WHO WE ARE

ISACOL S.A.S is a family company that was built with one goal, the complete satisfaction of our clients. We have spent almost half a century striving to meet and exceed the expectations of tens of thousands of ships and their owners. Our strong foundation is built by our family, our team, and making the two one ISACOL family. Every member of ISACOL has one mission; to meet our customer’s needs and complete the successful port call of every ship we have the honor to serve. Our founder, Captain Cesar Rojas, spent the beginning of his career as a naval officer in the Colombian Navy. He reached the highest level of sea Captain, before retiring to start ISACOL. He saw a need in Colombia’s growing oil, gas, and bulk markets for an agency that could combine military like organization, knowledge of local port and terminal requirements, and international standards into a World Class agency. Captain Rojas serves as the company’s President and CEO. He is pleased to see that as his family and his team continue to grow into leadership positions within the company, that ISACOL will grow and serve ships, their owners, and charterers for another 50 years and more.

OUR MISSION

To provide our clients with timely services, information, and logistics, so that they can have an efficient port call and successfully meet their business goals in Colombia.

OUR VISION

We strive every day to continue our position in Colombia as the premier tanker, gas, and chemical carrier ship agent. We know this only comes from our continued hard work on behalf of our clients.

OUR QUALITY STANDARD

As one of the first agencies in Colombia to achieve ISO 9001 status more than 20 years ago, ISACOL S.A.S. is committed to complying with the new ISO 9001:2015 and continuing to adapt and achieve as the international standards change and improve. ISACOL is implementing the new training programs and updating our equipment and facilities, as specified by ISO. As well, we continue to upgrade our systems and policies per our own company quality assurance programs.

SHIP AGENTS

With more than 40 years of experience in Colombia's ports, ISACOL is the country's leading agent for tankers, bulkers, and all types of cargo vessels.  We have the most dedicated and experienced team in Colombia, which enables us to meet and exceed our customers' needs and requirements.  Throughout our history, we have earned the trust of ship owners, charterers, terminals, and refineries, by performing not only to international standards such as ISO, but the high standards of our clients.  From major oil companies to one ship operators, ISACOL knows that on time information and operations is the key to a successful port call and the success of our clients.  We are here for you 24 hours a day 365 days a year.

PROTECTIVE AGENTS

As an owner's protective agent, we provide a full supervisory service to protect our client’s interests.  We accomplish this by having our team constantly monitor and review the charterer's agent, to help ensure they are meeting the port, terminal, and government regulations and policies.  We will oversee all port activities while your vessel is in port and keep you constantly up to date.

CONSULTANCY

Through our vast network and contacts, we can provide specialized consultancy services for shipping and trading companies.  From introductions to key players in Colombia, to data and informational research, we are your trusted source when looking for business and investment opportunities in Colombia.

HUSBANDRY

In its 40 plus years, ISACOL has developed excellent relationships with port, customs and immigration authorities.  As well, we have developed an expansive range of husbandry services with our trusted vendors and some direct ship services via ISACOL's offices throughout Colombia.  We are confident that we can meet your husbandry requirements and we will always strive for your 100% satisfaction. Some of our husbandry services include:

  • Customs clearance for spares and cargoes
  • Crew handling
  • In and out clearance
  • Communications
  • Bunker deliveries
  • Assistance as agents at local Dry docks
